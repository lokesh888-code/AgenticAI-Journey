## Learned
1) AI
Artificial Intelligence is the big umbrella. It means making computers do things that usually need human intelligence, like understanding language, recognizing images, making decisions, or solving problems. Think of AI as “smart machines.”

Example: A phone that unlocks with your face, or a chatbot that answers questions.

Memory trick: AI = “Can a machine act smart?”

2) Machine Learning
Machine Learning is a part of AI where computers learn from data instead of being told every rule by hand. The more data it sees, the better it can get at predicting or classifying things. Think of ML as “learning from examples.”

Example: Email spam filters learn from lots of emails to decide what is spam and what is not.

Memory trick: ML = “Show it many examples, and it learns the pattern.”

3) Deep Learning
Deep Learning is a more advanced part of machine learning that uses many-layered neural networks. It is especially good at working with huge amounts of data and complex things like images, audio, and language. Think of deep learning as “learning with layers.”

Example: Face recognition on your phone, or systems that detect objects in photos.

Memory trick: Deep Learning = “Many layers, deeper understanding.”

4) Generative AI
Generative AI is AI that creates new content instead of only classifying or predicting. It can generate text, images, music, code, and more by learning patterns from training data. Think of it as “AI that makes things.”

Example: A tool that writes an email draft, creates an image from a prompt, or generates code.

Memory trick: Generative AI = “Generate something new.”

5) Agentic AI
Agentic AI means AI that can plan, decide, and act toward a goal with some level of independence. Instead of only answering, it can break a task into steps, use tools, and keep working until the goal is done. Think of it as “AI that does.”

Example: An AI assistant that books a meeting, checks your calendar, drafts a message, and follows up automatically.

Memory trick: Agentic AI = “An agent that takes action.”


### To explain shortly
--------------
“AI, or Artificial Intelligence, is the broad field of making machines act intelligently, like understanding language, recognizing images, or making decisions.”

“Machine Learning is a subset of AI where machines learn from data instead of being manually programmed for every rule.”

“Deep Learning is a subset of machine learning that uses multi-layer neural networks, and it works especially well for complex tasks like image recognition, speech, and language.”

“Generative AI is a type of AI that creates new content such as text, images, code, or music based on what it has learned.”

“Agentic AI is AI that can take actions on its own to achieve a goal, such as planning steps, using tools, and completing tasks with less human guidance.”

Short interview version

“AI is the overall concept of making machines intelligent. Machine Learning is a part of AI where systems learn from data. Deep Learning is a more advanced form of ML that uses neural networks with many layers. Generative AI creates new content, while Agentic AI can plan and act to complete tasks autonomously.”

Agentic AI = takes actions to finish a goal.
